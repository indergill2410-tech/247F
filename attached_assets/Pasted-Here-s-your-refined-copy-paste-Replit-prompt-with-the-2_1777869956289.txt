## Important rule

Remove the phrase **“$2,000 covered value”** or any similar financial cover amount from **everywhere in public marketing copy across the app**.

That means:
- do **not** show it in the hero
- do **not** show it in the homepage membership section
- do **not** show it in pricing cards
- do **not** show it in CTA areas
- do **not** show it in badges, trust bullets, or banners

The exact financial cap or benefit limit should appear **only** inside:
- Terms & Conditions
- FAQ / fine print
- disclosure section
- legal details modal / expandable T&C block

Marketing claims should stay clean, clear, and emotionally persuasive, while detailed cover limits and legal constraints stay in supporting terms. Claims in advertising should be accurate and provable under Australian consumer law. [accc.gov](https://www.accc.gov.au/consumers/advertising-and-promotions)
