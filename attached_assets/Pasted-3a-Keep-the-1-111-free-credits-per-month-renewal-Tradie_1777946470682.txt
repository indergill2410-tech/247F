3a. Keep the 1,111 free credits per month renewal
Tradies continue to receive 1,111 free credits per month, automatically renewed. Do not remove or change this. Keep the wording "1111 credits / month — free" wherever it currently appears (on /partner, in tradie dashboard, in tradie onboarding).
3b. Replace flat 222-credits-per-claim with AI-sized per-job credit cost (with homeowner size-band guardrail)
Remove the "Each claim costs 222 credits" model entirely. Replace with a per-job credit cost determined when the homeowner posts the job, sized by an AI backend function and bounded by a homeowner-selected size band.
Add a "Job size" step to the homeowner post-job flow. Before final submission, the homeowner picks one of four bands:

Small — "A quick fix or minor task. Examples: leaking tap washer, paint a door, replace a power point, unblock a drain."
Medium — "Half a day's work or a single-room job. Examples: paint a room, install a dishwasher, repair a fence section, replace a hot water tap."
Large — "A multi-day project. Examples: full house exterior paint, retile a bathroom, rewire a room."
Premium — "A full renovation or major install. Examples: full house repaint, kitchen install, bathroom reno, full rewire."

This is the upper-bound guardrail. The AI cannot exceed the band the homeowner selected.
Build the AI sizing function as a backend endpoint. When a homeowner submits a job (after picking the size band), the backend calls an AI function (use the existing AI integration if present; otherwise OpenAI or Anthropic Claude API — there must be an env variable for the API key). Inputs: job title, description, trade category, homeowner-selected size band. Outputs:

estimated_credit_cost (integer, must fall within the homeowner's selected band)
job_size_category (string, matches the homeowner's selected band)
reasoning (string, internal/log only)

Credit cost bands:

Small: 30–60 credits
Medium: 80–150 credits
Large: 200–400 credits
Premium: 500–800 credits

If the AI returns a value outside the band, clamp to the nearest boundary. Store the credit cost on the job record at posting time. The cost is locked at post time and does not change after.
Display the credit cost prominently on each job card in the tradie's Browse Jobs feed: "45 credits to claim" with subtitle "Sized to job scope".

"1,111 free credits every month — automatically renewed for every active tradie."
"Credit cost varies by job — small jobs (a door repaint, a leaking tap) cost 30–60 credits. Large jobs (a full reno, a house repaint) cost up to 800 credits. The cost is shown upfront on every job card, before you claim."

3f. Tradie profile privacy — three tiers
Tradie full profiles must NOT be public. Implement three visibility tiers.
Tier 1 — Public / Anonymous (anyone unauthenticated, or signed-in users without a relevant context):
Tradie full profile pages are NOT accessible publicly. URL patterns like /tradie/[id] or /tradies/[name] should not exist as public pages, OR if they exist for SEO, redirect unauthenticated users to sign in. Tradies are not browsable or searchable to the general public.
Tier 2 — Trust Card view (homeowner who has posted a job and received a response from this tradie, but has not yet hired them):
Trust Card shows ONLY:

First name + last initial (e.g. "Mike R.")
Profile photo (if uploaded)
Primary trade + secondary trades (badges)
Star rating + total review count
Verified credentials ticks (ABN verified ✓ / Licence verified ✓ / Insurance confirmed ✓) — only show ticks that are actually verified, never fake
Average response time (e.g. "Usually replies within 1 hour")
Up to 3 most recent reviews — reviewer first name + last initial only, star rating, review text, date. No reviewer contact info.
Service area suburbs (general only, not exact address)
Their proposed quote / message for the current job

Trust Card does NOT show: full name, phone number, email, business address, ABN number itself, licence number itself, full review list, full portfolio, payment history, years on platform, total jobs completed count.
Trust Card includes the messaging: "You'll see [Mike R.]'s full contact details and business information once you accept their quote."
Tier 3 — Full Profile view (homeowner who has accepted this tradie's quote, AND admin):
Full Profile shows everything:

Full name
Phone number (click-to-call)
Email
Business name + ABN number
Licence number
Full review history
Portfolio / past work photos (if uploaded)
Full service area

Admin role always has full access to all tradie full profiles.
Tradie's "Edit Profile" page must include privacy notes per field group:

Public-but-limited ("Visible on Trust Card before hire"): first name + last initial, profile photo, trades, ratings, verified badges, suburb area.
Private-until-hired ("Visible only after a homeowner hires you"): full name, phone, email, ABN, licence number, full address, portfolio photos.
